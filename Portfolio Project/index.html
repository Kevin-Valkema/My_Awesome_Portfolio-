About Me

Allow me to introduce myself! My name is Kevin Valkema, a middle-aged father of four who enjoys craft beer, archery, and all things nerdy! I live in the gorgeous state of Michigan, where wildlife and wild places abound. Naturally, I grew up with a love of the outdoors, and a curiousity to match the unbridled forest within which I found myself frequenting. That curiosity has applied to everything that peaked my interest. Not least of which is technology. I have always been interested in the inner workings of things, and figuring out how to tear them apart and piece them back together. So, when I first discovered coding in my advanced age, 31, I wanted to know more. How do strings of data come together to create the content that permeates every aspect of our lives? Before I knew it I was enrolling in a boot camp hosted by Michigan State University. The learning is intensive, and challenging but, I am learning so much in such a short amount of time, and I cant wait to put my newly acquired knowledge to use, creating content to benefit the lives of many!
